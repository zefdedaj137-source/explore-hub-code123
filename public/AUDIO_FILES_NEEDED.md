## Message Received Sound Effect

Add the following audio file to the `/public` folder:

### `message-received.mp3`
- **Type:** Short notification sound
- **Duration:** 0.2-0.5 seconds
- **Mood:** Friendly, attention-grabbing, pleasant
- **Volume:** 60% (slightly louder than sent)
- **Suggestions:**
  - Clear "ding" or "chime"
  - Soft "bell" sound
  - Pleasant "notification tone"
  - Gentle "ping" effect
- **Note:** Should be distinct from sent message sound
- **Example sources:**
  - Notification Sounds (https://notificationsounds.com)
  - Zapsplat (https://www.zapsplat.com)
  - Freesound (https://freesound.org)
